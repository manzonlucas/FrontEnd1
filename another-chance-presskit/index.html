Biography

Hi! We are Another Chance, a melodic hardcore/punk band from Rosario, Argentina, formed in 2008. Our music is distinguished for having fast technical riffs plus a mix between melodic voices and hardcore screaming. We released our first album in 2014 and played it on international shows with great bands of the genre like Mute (Canada) and Forus (France), among several shows with local bands from our country. Now, we have just finished recording our 2nd album, which is going to be released soon this 2021.
